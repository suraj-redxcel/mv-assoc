CS Vishal Bhosle

M. Com, LL.B, F.C.S.

CS Vishal Bhosale is a highly accomplished professional with a Post Graduate degree in Commerce, a Law Graduate, and a Fellow Member of the Institute of Company Secretaries of India (ICSI). With 18 years of experience in full-time practice, he has developed expertise in a wide range of corporate legal matters, including Company Law, Secretarial Audits, and complex legal issues related to corporate governance.

Throughout his career, CS Vishal Bhosale has been involved in providing expert consultancy to large companies, handling secretarial audits, liaising with government offices, and addressing critical corporate legal matters such as winding up and resolving corporate disputes. His deep understanding of corporate law has made him a go-to consultant for various startups and Section 8 companies (non-profit organizations) across India.

Key Areas of Practice and Consultancy:

  • SME IPOs: Advising Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs) on preparation for Initial Public Offerings (IPOs) and navigating the regulatory process.

  • Startup Consultancy: Assisting startups with legal, regulatory, and compliance requirements, ensuring they are positioned for growth and success.

  • Company Takeover Due Diligence: Conducting comprehensive due diligence during company takeovers to ensure smooth and legally sound transactions.

  • Legal Due Diligence: Conducting thorough legal due diligence to ensure compliance and minimize risks for businesses during corporate transactions.

  • Intellectual Property Rights (IPR): Offering consultancy in managing Intellectual Property Rights (patents, trademarks, copyrights, etc.) and protecting business assets.

  • Drafting Agreements & Opinions: Drafting legal agreements and providing legal opinions on complex issues under the Companies Act and other related laws.
