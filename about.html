About us.

We are a firm of Practicing Company Secretaries

MV is a team of professionals with integrity and confidence having experience in the related field for almost 7years. The firm is providing accurate, timely and high-end solutions to complex corporate issues including Corporate Laws, Intellectual Property Laws, Statutory Legal Compliances, Joint Ventures, Collaborations, Foreign Direct Investment, subsidiary companies etc.

Apart from this, MV had granted Registered Service Mark by the trademark registry to ensure clients' trust and confidence at all times which is our ultimate aim.
