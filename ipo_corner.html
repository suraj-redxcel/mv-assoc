IPO Corner.

IPO (Intial Public Offer) SME AND MAIN BOARD

  1. Consultation and Monitoring on the compliances required under the Companies Act, 2013, SEBI (LODR), The Securities Contracts (Regulation) Act, 1956 (‘SCRA’) and the rules made there under, The Depositories Act, 1996 and the Regulations and Bye-laws framed there under, Securities Contracts (Regulation) Rules, 1957, The Securities and Exchange Board of India (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2009 and such other act applicable for Listing and Initial Public Offer;

  2. Documentation for Conversion of Shares in to Demat of equity shares of the Company and getting the ISIN;

  3. Documentation follow-up and approvals for Conversion of Company into Public Limited (All ROC filing and documents);

  4. Verifying the composition of the Board of Directors (proper balance of Executive Directors, Non-Executive Directors and Independent Directors and any change in the composition during the period) Compliance with corporate governance requirements under SEBI LODR Regulations, Constitution of Board of Director as per Company Act 2013 and LODR;

  5. Provide assistances in evaluation and identification of Compliances as and when requires as per the applicable Laws;

  6. Constitution of Various Committees of the Board as per LODR and Company Act 2013;

  7. Secretarial Due Diligence before IPO Process;

  8. Review and make necessary Changes in MOA and AOA;

  9. Review of Outstanding convertible instruments, and Allotment work, if any;

  10. Increase of Authorized Share Capital of the Company and filing of all e-forms and comply the Provisions related thereto;

  11. Preparation and e-form GNL -1 for filing of Prospectus in with Registrar of Company and provide all assistance to comply rules and regulation applicable there to;

  12. To Represent before the various Government Authorities such as Registrar of Company, Regional Director, Central Government, as and when required on behalf of the Company for compliance related to Initial Public Offer;

  13. Filing, Certification and verify the all documents of returns and e-forms to be filed with Registrar of Companies as and when required;

  14. Filing of Return of Allotments with Registrar of Company and comply the all rules and regulations of the company Act 2013;

  15. Consultation and issue of opinion on various company law matters and those related to various SEBI Regulations’ applicable for IPO Process as and when required.
